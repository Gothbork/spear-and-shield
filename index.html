📜 Combat Codex
Combat happens on the map. Tap an action, then a direction.

🎯 Two-tap combat — Tap 🗡️ Thrust / 🛡 Bash / ✦ Feint / 💨 Sprint to arm it (button glows gold; the four neighbor tiles light up). Then tap a direction on the D-pad to commit. Tap the ✦ center to cancel.

🚶 Walking into a foe is blocked — Use Thrust or Bash to attack a tile.

⏳ Turn-based — Every action (move, attack, brace, wait) ends your turn. Enemies then move and attack. Adjacent enemies telegraph their next strike (red pulsing border + text banner) — Brace the heavy ones.

⚔ Weapon Traits:

🗡️ Spear (pierce) — Ignores 50% enemy DEF.
⚔️ Sword (balanced) — +1 bonus damage per hit.
🔨 Mace (crush) — Ignores 30% DEF; bonus stun on bash and small stun on thrust.
🪓 Axe (cleave, 2H) — Inflicts Bleed on hit.
⚡ Greatsword (sweep, 2H) — +25% damage per status on the foe.

🛡 Brace — Free action; +shield, +4 stam, blocks 85% of next hit. ⏸ Wait — free; +3 stam, do nothing.

✦ Feint — Next Thrust crits guaranteed.

💨 Sprint — Dash 2 tiles (3 stam). Loud — enemies get an extra turn. Can't sprint while bleeding.

🔥 Rest / 👁 Scout — Rest deeply restores HP+stam (passes 2 turns; can't with enemies adjacent). Scout reveals foes beyond your sight (1 stam).

⚠ Enemies hunt — Wolves prowl forests, goblins guard camps, shamans keep distance, alpha rallies the pack.

☠ Poison 4 HP/turn · 🩸 Bleed 3 HP/turn, no shield regen, no sprint.

🔮 SHAMAN & SPIRIT REALM

✨ Starting Familiar — Shaman begins with one random familiar spirit bound, granting an active spell:

🔥 Fire Bolt (Ember Wisp) — 4-tile ranged spell, 10-16 dmg. 3 stam, CD 2.
🛡 Ward (Guardian Wisp) — +18 shield instant. 3 stam, CD 3.
💚 Mend (Mending Wisp) — Heal 20 HP. 3 stam, CD 3.

Spells appear as purple buttons. Bolts arm directionally — tap, then choose direction. Ward & Mend cast on yourself instantly.

🌀 Realm — Shaman starts with Spirit Vision. Tap 🌀 Realm to cross over. The world dims and spirits become solid. Living enemies fade out; chests can't be opened.

🪄 Spirit weapons — Ritual Staff (start) or Spirit Staff. Deal 2× damage to spirits, half to flesh. Mortal weapons do half damage to spirits.

✨ Subdue — When a spirit drops to ≤30% HP (marked ✨), arm Subdue and tap its direction. It binds to one of your 4 slots, granting its power.

🐾 Pack Hunt (Wolf) — Thrust costs -1 stamina.
👁 Cunning (Goblin) — +10% crit.
☠ Hex Touch (Shaman) — Thrusts apply Poison.
⚔ War Cry (Warchief) — +4 ATK above 50% HP.
🩸 Trollblood (Troll Shade) — Regen 2 HP/turn.

☠ Death as Spirit — A Shaman who falls becomes a ghost (1 chance only). Hunt and bind a spirit to be reborn — or be lost forever.

✨ Spirit Dust / Scrolls — Warriors can find these to gain temporary or permanent Spirit Vision. Scrolls also unlock 2 spirit slots.
